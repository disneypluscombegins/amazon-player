How can I use Amazon mytv enter code?


To activate Amazon Prime Video on your smart TV or streaming device using Amazon mytv enter code, launch the Prime Video app to receive an 8-digit code. On a phone or computer, visit amazon.com/mytv, log in to your Amazon account, and enter the code. Your device will be registered, allowing you to start streaming instantly.

How to use Amazon mytv enter your code?

  • Turn on your smart TV or streaming device and open the Amazon Prime Video app.
  • Select the option to sign in or register, and an 8-digit activation code will appear on your screen.
  • On a separate device (phone or computer), go to amazon.com/mytv.
  • Sign in with your Amazon account credentials.
  • Enter the 8-digit code displayed on your TV or device screen into the field on amazon.com/mytv.
  • Click “Register Device” to complete the setup.
  • Your TV or streaming device will refresh, and you can now access Prime Video content.
